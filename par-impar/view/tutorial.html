Instructions

- Starting the Game: The player with the odd numbers always starts first by placing one of their numbers in any empty square on the grid.

- Taking Turns: Players take turns placing one of their numbers into any open square.

- No Repeats: Each number can only be used once.

- Winning: The goal is to be the first player to place a number in a row, column, or diagonal (main or secondary) so that the sum of that number and the two numbers already in that line equals exactly 15. You can only win if there are three numbers in the line.
